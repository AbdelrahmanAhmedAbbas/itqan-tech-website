# Itqan Tech Landing

## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary audience:** Founders and startup leaders with validated SaaS ideas who need a technical partner to build production-ready products
- **Context:** They've likely been burned by agencies before. They're evaluating Itqan against other dev shops and freelancers. They're spending $15K-$80K and need confidence before committing
- **Job to be done:** Find a trustworthy, senior technical team that can turn their idea into a scalable product — fast, without the typical agency runaround
- **Bilingual audience:** English and Arabic speakers (full RTL support), serving clients across 3+ countries

### Brand Personality
- **3 words:** Precise, Elegant, Trusted
- **Voice:** Confident but never arrogant. Direct, no-fluff language. Speaks like a senior engineer who respects your time
- **Tone:** Calm authority — we've done this before, and we'll do it right
- **Name meaning:** "Itqan" (إتقان) means "mastery" or "perfection" in Arabic — the brand name IS the promise

### Emotional Goals
- **Primary:** "I can trust these people" — calm confidence, clarity, professionalism that removes doubt
- **Secondary:** "Finally, someone who gets it" — relief and connection
- **NOT:** Hype, urgency, or flashy showmanship. Never "growth-hack bro" energy

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Dark precision meets premium whitespace (Linear/Vercel + Apple/Stripe)
- **Color strategy:** Dark hero (Void) transitions to light body (Snow). Accent blue used sparingly
- **Typography:** Inter (clarity), Playfair Display italic (editorial), JetBrains Mono (technical). Arabic: IBM Plex Sans Arabic + Noto Naskh Arabic
- **Shape language:** Large border-radius (2rem) cards, rounded-full buttons. Soft but not playful
- **Motion:** GSAP-powered, purposeful. Never gratuitous
- **Anti-references:** No Framer gradients, no bouncy energy, no cluttered templates, no stock photography

### Design Principles
1. **Precision over decoration** — Every element earns its place
2. **Quiet confidence** — Let the work speak. Restrained color, generous whitespace
3. **Clarity is respect** — Clear hierarchy, scannable content, obvious next steps
4. **Craft in the details** — Micro-interactions, consistent spacing, thoughtful transitions
5. **Bilingual-native** — RTL is not an afterthought

### Accessibility
- **Target:** WCAG AA
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for GSAP animations
- 4.5:1 contrast for text, 3:1 for large text/UI
- Keyboard navigation, semantic HTML, meaningful alt text
